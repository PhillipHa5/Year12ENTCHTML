Help

Help, the system's elements are out of place!

Even though the system is dimensionally responsive, the recommended resolution is 1920x1080, if you are able to change it, this is how.Ensure that youre app scaling is set to 100% in your computer's settings. You can change your scale by going into your computers's Settings > System > Display and on the Display page there will be a Scale and layout drop down box, ensure that the scale is set to 100%, and the display resolution is set to 1920x1080.

Help, the form is not accepting my submissions!

Ensure that the data you input is correct, and that the dates are inputted correctly, as the system does not accept borrow dates that occur after the return date

Help, none of the data is loading in the tables, nor the graphs!

Ensure that you are connecetd to a stable internet connection and try reloading the page.
